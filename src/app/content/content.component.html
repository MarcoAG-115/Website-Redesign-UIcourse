About

I am an Associate Professor in the department of Computer Science and Software Engineering in the Samuel Ginn College of Engineering at Auburn University. My research focuses primarily on software visualization and how it can be applied to both computing education and software engineering. I teach courses in computing fundamentals, software engineering, and cloud computing. I also coordinate the curricula, assessment, and accreditation of our department's three undergraduate programs.
